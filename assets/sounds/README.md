# RaiderPark Notification Sounds

Add your notification sounds here.

## Required Files

- `notification.wav` - Default notification sound (referenced in app.json)

## Recommended Specifications

- Format: WAV (for cross-platform compatibility)
- Sample Rate: 44100 Hz
- Bit Depth: 16-bit
- Duration: 0.5-2 seconds
- Style: Clean, professional chime

## Free Sound Resources

1. [Zapsplat](https://www.zapsplat.com) - Free notification sounds
2. [Freesound](https://freesound.org) - Creative Commons sounds
3. [Notification Sounds](https://notificationsounds.com) - Free notification tones

## Apple Design Guidelines

For iOS, notification sounds should:
- Be short (under 30 seconds, ideally under 5 seconds)
- Not be annoying or jarring
- Be distinct enough to recognize
